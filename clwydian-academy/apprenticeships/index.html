The difference

Same framework. Half the drag.

An apprenticeship in Wales comes with clear requirements — framework content, assessment standards, workplace hours — and we deliver every one of them. What we refuse to do is pad the journey. Traditional providers run apprenticeships on a day-release drip across two years; our structured blocks, distance learning and in-house quality assurance move you through the same framework at the fastest compliant pace.

That speed is the whole point of the academy: a qualified, productive professional on the salon floor sooner — earning properly, progressing sooner, and costing the industry less drift.

  • Framework-compliant delivery, documented for employers and funders
  • Cohorts capped at ten — apprentices get the same coached intensity as every Clwydian learner
  • Digital portfolio evidence with internal quality assurance and unannounced workplace sampling
  • A dedicated point of contact for the employer, not a call centre
