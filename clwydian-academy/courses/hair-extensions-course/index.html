The four Mondays

  • Week 1 — luxury brand takeover day. Learn directly from an elite brand educator: philosophy, colour matching and technical application on premium hair systems
  • Week 2 — advanced cutting and blending. The finish is the secret: texturising and seamless blending so the work looks completely natural
  • Week 3 — speed, maintenance and business. Faster application, safe removal and re-fitting, and the consultation strategy that sells high-ticket packages
  • Week 4 — live-model assessment. A complete installation and style under VTCT assessor supervision to clear your qualification

Why extensions

The highest-earning service on the modern price list.

One extension client a week can transform monthly takings — and clients pay a premium for stylists whose training carries a regulated qualification rather than a one-day certificate.

We welcome independent stylists future-proofing a freelance business and whole teams from established salons standardising their luxury offer.

  • Dual certification: regulated VTCT unit + prestige brand credential
  • Unlocks a premium trade account with the partner brand
  • Seamless progression to our upcoming Level 3 creative extension units
  • Small cohorts for intensive hands-on tutor support
