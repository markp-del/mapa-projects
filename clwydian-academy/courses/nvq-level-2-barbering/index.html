Fast-track · Beginners & cross-mapping hairdressers

NVQ Level 2 Barbering, in six months.

The barbering industry is booming — precision fades, sharp styling and luxury grooming. Whether you’re new to the trade or a qualified hairdresser adding a lucrative second string, our fast-track diploma takes you there in six months, not two years.

What you’ll master

  • The professional barbering consultation — hair and scalp analysis, face-shape mapping, growth patterns and contraindications
  • Precision men’s cutting — clipper work, scissor-over-comb, texturising and contemporary skin fades
  • Facial hair design — tapering, shaping and cutting beards and moustaches to the client’s features
  • Shampooing, conditioning and treating varied hair and scalp conditions
  • Blow-drying, structural styling and grooming products for modern men’s looks
  • Three specialist add-on units most entry courses skip: an introduction to luxury wet shaving, the business of barbering, and retail product promotion

A smarter route in

Six months. Not one year at college, not two on day-release.

Traditional college barbering demands three days a week for a year; standard apprenticeships stretch two years at a day a week. Our hybrid model — two intensive academy days each fortnight, theory at home between — gets you qualified and earning in six months.

Already a hairdresser? Cross-mapping recognises the units you hold, such as consultation and shampooing, cutting the repetition and accelerating your route to the chair.

  • Coached by an active industry practitioner with deep roots in the regional salon and barbershop circuit
  • Competition-ready portfolio built during your practical sessions — with coaching toward WorldSkills UK and Welsh grooming championships
  • Progression routes into NVQ Level 2 Hairdressing, Level 3, and graduate pathways into the Charlotte Eleanor Group franchise network
  • Strictly ten learners per intake for one-to-one coaching
