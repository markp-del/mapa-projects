The three pillars

  • Advanced hair and scalp analysis — the scientific knowledge to diagnose complex conditions confidently and advise with authority
  • Lifestyle and environmental mapping — reading routines, water type, heat-styling habits and budget to curate genuinely bespoke recommendations
  • Non-aggressive selling — psychological communication frameworks that make recommendations a natural extension of client care, never a pitch

The four Mondays

From science to showcase.

Week 1 · Scientific foundations

Advanced trichological indicators — spotting hidden hair and scalp issues and matching them precisely to product chemistry.

Week 2 · Behavioural consultation

Active listening, facial profiling and lifestyle analysis; proposing high-value services with confidence.

Week 3 · The retail masterclass

Structuring product instruction, demonstrating home-care regimes, cross-selling services and unblocking the mindset that caps your income.

Week 4 · Practical evaluation & graduation

A live, comprehensive consultation and product-instruction showcase under VTCT assessor guidance.
