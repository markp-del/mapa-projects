CPD · VTCT Level 2 Award · 1 day

The most important day of training you’ll ever book.

Close-contact professionals see what others can’t. Parting hair at the chair, working on the scalp and neck, treating skin — you are uniquely placed to spot the early warning signs of skin cancer. This one-day, knowledge-based VTCT award turns that position into potentially life-saving vigilance.

What you’ll learn in a day

  • The main types of skin cancer, including melanoma and non-melanoma variations
  • The ABCDE detection framework — asymmetry, borders, colours, diameters and evolving moles
  • High-risk zones mapped to your industry — the scalp and neck for hair professionals, limbs for therapists
  • Sensitive communication — how to advise a client to seek medical attention calmly, professionally and without alarm

Why certify your whole team

A badge of trust your clients can feel.

Advertising that every member of staff is certified in skin cancer awareness builds a level of clinical trust few salons can match — and extends genuine care beyond the treatment itself.

For hair professionals, the award is also a natural first step toward advanced study in trichology and scalp health.

  • Purely knowledge-based: turn up, learn, walk away qualified
  • Team discounts make whole-business certification affordable
  • Universally accessible — no prerequisites of any kind
  • Professional, practical guidance — this course trains awareness and signposting, never diagnosis
