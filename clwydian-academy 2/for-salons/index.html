For salon owners · North Wales & the border

We train your apprentices. You keep your columns full.

Three in four salons say recruitment is getting worse, and in-salon training eats senior time you can’t spare. Clwydian Academy exists to carry that load for the whole region — training your people to VTCT NVQ standard while they work for you.

Apprentices

Outsourced apprentice training

Apprenticeships are a core component of the academy. Your apprentice trains with us in structured practical blocks under the Welsh apprenticeship framework and returns salon-ready at the fastest pace the framework allows — not the two years colleges take. Training is fully funded for eligible apprentices; you pay the wage, never the course.

Your team

CPD for established staff

Extensions, perming, relaxing, consultation-and-retail and business masterclasses — regulated VTCT units that add services to your price list in weeks, not terms — plus one-day skin cancer awareness certification for the whole team — with priority scheduling for whole-team bookings. Browse CPD courses.

Pipeline

Qualified hires, ready sooner

Our open cohorts graduate work-ready adults who chose this trade deliberately. Salons registered with us hear first when graduates are looking for chairs in your area.

Why partner with us

Complementary to colleges. Built for commerce.

Further education does an important job for school-leavers. We deliberately serve a different need: adult learners, compressed timelines, and the commercial skills — consultation, rebooking, pricing — that make a trainee profitable in your salon, not just qualified on paper.

Training is delivered by education-qualified senior practitioners with internal quality assurance run in-house, in a purpose-fitted St Asaph academy with professional-grade stations and COSHH-compliant ventilation.
