NVQ Level 3: from stylist to senior stylist.

Level 3 is the progression every ambitious hairdresser ultimately needs — the advanced diploma behind premium pricing, creative authority and leadership roles. We deliver it the same way we deliver everything: intensively, in six months.

What you’ll master

  • Creative and precision cutting — advanced shape, texture and personalised looks
  • Colour correction — diagnosing and resolving the work other stylists can’t
  • Advanced colouring — creative placement, balayage-style techniques and tonal control
  • Technical services — smoothing, restructuring and specialist treatments
  • Client leadership — consultation at senior level, building and pricing a premium column
  • The Commercial Resilience Module at Level 3 depth — margins, rebooking economics and growing beyond a single chair

Who it’s for

Made for the stylist stuck at “good”.

You’re qualified. You’re busy. But the premium work — the corrections, the transformations, the bridal and editorial jobs — keeps going elsewhere. Level 3 is the difference between doing hair and being sought out for it, and it’s the credential salons look for when they hand over seniority, training responsibility or the front of their price list.

For self-employed and mobile stylists, Level 3 typically supports a meaningful step up in average ticket price — it pays for itself, then keeps paying.

Level 2 makes you a hairdresser. Level 3 makes you the hairdresser people ask for by name.
