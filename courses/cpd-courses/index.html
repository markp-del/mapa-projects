How CPD runs

Built around a working column.

Courses run one to five days, scheduled so you lose the minimum of earning time. Groups stay small, models are live, and every course closes with the commercial piece: what to charge, how to launch the service, and how to fill it.

Dates and pricing are published per course as the academy opens — register now and we’ll contact you first when your course is scheduled.
