The foundation diploma of the profession — compressed into an intensive, live-model programme for adults. No experience needed. Leave qualified, confident and commercially ready.

Course at a glance

Qualification
NVQ Level 2 Diploma in Hairdressing VTCT-standard, nationally recognised
Duration
6 months, rolling cohorts of 10
Format
2 practical days per week at Deeside Theory online via our digital learning environment
Entry
Adults 19+ · no prior experience required
Fees
£4,500 self-funded Free for eligible Welsh adults via the Personal Learning Account
Next intake
Founding cohort — applications open late 2026, training begins early 2027
Centre status
VTCT Approved Centre application in progress Approval precedes the first cohort; PLA drawdown follows Medr registration

What you’ll master

  • Cutting — classic and contemporary shapes for women’s and men’s hair
  • Colouring and lightening — full head, regrowth, foils and toning, with COSHH-safe chemical practice
  • Styling and finishing — blow-drying, setting and occasion work
  • Consultation and client care — analysis, advice and building a returning column
  • Salon professionalism — health & safety, hygiene and working under real appointment pressure
  • The Commercial Resilience Module — pricing, marketing, digital booking and money management for your first 90 days of earning

Why this works when evening classes don’t.

All practice, no padding

Theory happens online before you arrive. Every academy hour goes on scissors-in-hand, live-model work — the hours that actually build a hairdresser.

NVQ assessment is continuous and practical, evidenced in a digital portfolio with in-house quality assurance — no single exam day deciding your future.

Earning from day one

Graduates leave with progression routes mapped: employed roles, chair rental, or a supported mobile business through our wider group.
