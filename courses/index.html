How every course works

Practical on site. Theory around your life.

Physical academy hours are reserved entirely for live-model practical work — the thing you can’t learn from a screen. Theory, testing and portfolio evidence run through our digital learning environment, on your schedule. It’s how six months does the work of two years.

  • Cohorts capped at ten learners — every session is coached, never crowded
  • Live models from the first weeks, real appointment conditions throughout
  • VTCT-standard NVQ assessment with in-house internal quality assurance
  • Commercial Resilience Module on every course — pricing, marketing, booking tech and money
  • Progression routes into employment, chair rental or a supported mobile business
